Pizza recipe

This recipe of pizza is not an international one but more of a home made one.

It is simple and tasty!

Ingredients

  • tomato
  • dough
  • garlic
  • butter
  • cheese
  • toppings of your like

    • Steps

    1. Prepare the dough for your pizza and let it settle for a while
    2. Shape your dough the way you like it
    3. Spread butter all over it
    4. Chop some tomatos and put them on top of your soon to be pizza ;)
    5. Put some cheese on it and put it in the oven
