Toast recipe

This is a simple toast recipe that anyone can make

Its a must try !!

Ingredients

  • sliced bread
  • butter
  • egg

Steps

  1. Spread butter over your bread then put it in a toaster for a minute
  2. Mix your eggs up and put some salt in it
  3. Take out the toasted bread and dip it into the egg mix
  4. Put it on hot pan and take it out when it is ready
