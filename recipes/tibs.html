Tibs recipe

This is a recipe for a cultural food of Ethiopia called Tibs.

Its easy to make and it is so tasty !!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • butter
  • Oregano powder

Steps

  1. Chop the onion and put it on a hot pan then add some butter on it
  2. After a while mix the ground beef with the caramalized onion
  3. Wait until the beef changes its color then add some more butter
  4. Take it out and serve it with bread or injera
